Spring
公達に狐化けたり宵の春
kindachi ni / kitsune bake tari / yoi no haru
into a young nobleman, / the fox has transformed himself / on a spring evening
筋違にふとん敷きたり宵の春
sujikai ni / futon shiki tari / yoi no haru
at a slant, / the bedding is laid out / on a spring evening
のうれんに東風吹くいせの出店かな
nōren ni / kochi fuku ise no / dedana kana
through the curtains / the east wind blows, Ise / shop-stalls
春雨や小磯の小貝ぬるるほど
harusame ya / koiso no kogai / nururu hodo
spring rain― / until little shells on the beach / are moist
折り釘に烏帽子かけたり春の宿
ori kugi ni e / boshi kake tari / haru no yado
on a bent nail / a court-cap hangs; / a lodging in spring
指南車を胡地に引去る霞かな
shinansha o / kochi ni hikisaru / kasumi kana
the guide-cart of heaven / dragged off to the northern lands / beyond the haze
Summer
にほひある衣も畳まず春の暮
nioi aru / kinu mo tatamazu / haru no kure
the perfumed / robe, left unfolded; / spring dusk
