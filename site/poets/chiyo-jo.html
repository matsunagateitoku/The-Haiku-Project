Selected poems
稲妻の　尾や其のまゝに　かきつばた
inazuma no / o ya sono mama ni / kakitsubata
the lightning's tail — / just as it is, / an iris
Summer · 杜若 kakitsubata (iris)
朝顔に　釣瓶とられて　もらひ水
asagao ni / tsurube torarete / morai mizu
the morning glory / has captured the bucket — / i'll beg water elsewhere
Autumn · 朝顔 asagao (morning glory)
夕顔や　女子の肌の　見ゆる時
yūgao ya / joshi no hada no / miyuru toki
the evening glory — / the moment / a girl's skin shows
Autumn · 夕顔 yūgao (moonflower; evening glory)
落鮎や　日に日に水の　しづまる磯かな
ochiayu ya / hi ni hi ni mizu no / shizumaru iso kana
the falling ayu — / day by day the water / settling at the shore
Autumn · 落ち鮎 ochi-ayu (descending sweetfish)
