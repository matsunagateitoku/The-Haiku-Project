松永貞徳  ·  49 poems
Spring
霞さへまだらにたつやとらの年
kasumi sae / madara ni tatsu ya / tora no toshi
even the mist / is decked in stripes / year of the tiger
花よりも団子やありて帰る雁
hana yori mo / dango ya arite / kaeru kari
It is said, dumplings / are better than blossoms- / returning geese.
紅梅やかの銀公がからごろも
kōbai ya / kano ginkō no / Karakoromo
crimson plum— / renowned Empress Ginko’s / Chinese robe
比翼かやはねをならべて帰雁
hiyoku kaya / hane wo narabete / kaeru kari
be as two birds! / flying wingtip to wingtip / returning geese
野寺あれて跡にやはゆる仏の座
nodera arete / ato ni ya wa yuru / hotoke no za
the country temple decays— / and in its place a lotus / burns warmly on
かははがれからきめやみる山椒の木
kawa wa gare / kara ki me ya miru / sansho no ki
the river runs dry— / looking at the withered buds / of the pepper tree
信あれば是も飛梅の奇特かな
shin areba / kore mo tobi ume no / kitoku kana
with true faith / even this is a miracle / of the flying plum
かへり点の文字か跡先帰雁
kaeriten no / moji ka ato saki / kaerikari
diacritical marked / letters? Switching order / returning geese
在次第うす雪きやせ今日の春
ari shidai / usu yuki kiyase / kyō no haru
melt every last bit / of the dappled snow / this day of spring
ありたつたひとりたつたる今年かな
ari tattta / hitori tattaru / kotoshi kana
Oh! He stood! / He is standing by himself! / This New Year
大服や城を粉になす茶磨山
ōfuku ya / shiro wo ko ni nasu / chausuyama
auspicious New Year’s tea— / the castle is ground to powder / from Mt. Tea Mortar
しほるるは何かあんずの花の色
shioruru wa / nanka annzu no / hana no iro
withering / with worry, the hue / of apricot blossoms
ねぶらせて養ひたてよ花のあめ
neburasete / yashinai-tate yo / hana no ame
raise them up / by letting them lick / the candy rain of spring
春たつは衣の棚の霞かな
haru tatsu wa / koromo no tana no / kasumi kana
spring begins / in the Robe Bureau district / with bolts cut from mist
ゆき尽くす江南の春の光かな
end of the snow journy / the light of spring in Jiangnan / how it shines!
Yuki tsukusu / Kōnan no haru no / hikari kana
もえながら有るや火鼠のかは柳
moe nagara / aru ya hinezumi no / kawa yanagi
While burning / the fire rat skin / river willow.
玉札の飛脚か急ぎ帰雁
tamazusa no / hikyaku ka isogi / kaeru kari
a jade-letter courier— / surely it hurries past / the returning wild geese
Summer
烏には似ぬうの花ぞ鷺の色
karasu niwa / ninu unohana zo / sagi no iro
resembling a crow / not in the least, the cormorant flower / color of egret
ねふらせて養ひ立よ花の雨
nefurasete / yashihai tate yo / hana no ame
let it sleep / and nurse it into growth— / the blossom rain
すそ野暑く頭寒足熱富士の雪
suso no atsuku / zukan sokunetsu / fuji no yuki
these foothills are so hot / cool the head and heat the feet / Mt. Fuji
雪月花一度に見する卯木かな
setsu-getsu-ka / ichido ni misuru / utsuki kana
snow, moon, and blossoms / viewed all at once / in deutzia flowers
山の腰にはく夕だちや雲の帯
yama no koshi ni / haku yudachi ya / kumo no obi
from mountain hips / hangs an evening storm blade— / belt of clouds
Autumn
暑き夜の星はあせぼかあまの原
atsuki yo no / hoshi wa asebo ka / amano hara
on this hot night / are the stars sweating too / up in the sky-plain?
正月にうちしは夢か玉まつり
shogatsu ni / uchi shi wa yume ka / tama matsuri
struck at New Year— / was it a dream? / the spirit festival
つづりさせとちちち鳴くやきれぎれす
tsuzuri sase to / chichichi naku ya / kiregiresu
"patch it, patch it" / so chirps the cricket, / over and over
おもしろくはらはら落る木葉哉
omoshiroku / harahara ochiru / konoha kana
delightfully / scattering and falling— / the fallen leaves
七夕のなかうどなれや宵の月
tanabata no / nakaudo nare ya / yoi no tsuki
be the matchmaker / for the star festival— / evening moon
皆人のひるねのたねや秋のつき
mina hito no / hirune no tane ya / aki no tsuki
the seed of / everyone's naps— / autumn moon
酒や時雨のめば紅葉ぬ人もなし
sake ya shigure / nomeba momijinu / hito mo nashi
wine & winter drizzle / drink it up and everyone's face / reddens like maple leaves
歌いづれ小町躍や伊勢躍
uta izure / komachi odori ya / ise odori
which has the better songs? / the Komachi dance- / or the Ise dance
てん長くち人誉むるや秋の月
ten nagaku / chihito homuru ya / aki no tsuki
heavenly marked and / celebrated by the multitudes— / moon of autumn
くくり目を見つつ夜長き枕かな

Winter
つみ綿かぬり桶なりの庭の雪
tsumi wata ka / nuri oke nari no / niwa no yuki
is it pulled cotton? / a lacquered bucket covered with snow / in the garden
冬籠虫けらまでもあなかしこ
fuyugomori / mushikera made mo / ana kashiko
winter seclusion / even down to the lowliest insects, / how reverent!
先かづく頭はかみなづきんかな
mazu ka zuku / kashira wa kamina / zukin kana
first must wear / on a baldhead in godless month / a hood
しも月のあるに付てやかみな月
shimo tsuki no / aru ni tsukite ya / kaminatsuki
the frost month / is below and so above / is the godless month
New Year
けさたるるつららやよだれうしの年
kesa taruru / tsurara ya yodare / ushi no toshi
• / • / •
松ならび穴へ餅ひき子のひ哉
matsu narabi / ana e mochi hiki / ko no hi kana
rows of pine trees— / into the hole they pull the mochi / this Children's Day
正月の礼者とながむいぬの年
shogatsu no / reija to nagamu / inu no toshi
New Year's visitors— / I watch them come and go / in the Year of the Dog
つくつくし売るやはかまの町くだり
tsukushi uru ya / hakama no machi / kudari
horsetails for sale— / down the street of hakama / shops they go
けさたるるつららやよだれうしの年
• / • / •
• / • / •
梅も先にほひてくるや午の年
ume mo saki / nioite kuru ya / ushi no toshi
The scent of plum / comes first carried by / the Year of the Ox
New year
鳳凰も出でよのどけきとりの年
hōō mo ide / nodokeki / tori no toshi
may even phoenixes / come out this calm / year of the bird
summer
門前に市も立花の盛哉
kado mae ni / ichi mo tachibana no / sakari kana
a throng of mandarin viewers / gather before my gate / like a bazaar
winter
春と夏と秋とけふとの四ぐれかな
haru to natsu to / aki to kyō to no / shigure kana
spring & summer & / autumn & today’s / makes “four kures”
足はやき雲や時雨の先ばしり
ashihayaki / kumo ya shigure no / Sakibashiri
the quick-footed / cloud—winter shower's / forerunner
きつくふるをやいたやとゆふ時雨
Hard falling sound / on the roof boards, “it hurts!” / says the winter shower
kutsuku furu / oto ya itaya to / yuu shigure
山姥が尿やしぐれの山めぐり
the mt. Hag / goes around the mt. / Pissing a winter shower
yamauba ga / bari ya shigure no / Yamamuguri
時宗寺の時雨の亭や雨やどり
＠ the winter rain pavilion / @ Jishū Temple / waiting for the rain to stop
Jishūdera no / shigure no chin ya / Ameyadori
