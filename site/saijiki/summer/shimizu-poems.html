清水 Shimizu
 ·  3 poems
さざれ蟹　足這いのぼる　清水かな
Sazare gani / ashi hai noboru / shimizu kana
A little crab crawls up my foot — pure water.
Basho
源は氷か雪か　苔清水
Minamoto wa / kori ka yuki ka / koke shimizu
Is its source ice, or snow? Moss pure water.
Taimu
其底に木葉　散ふる　清水かな
Sono soko ni / konoha toshifuru / shimizu kana
At the bottom, tree leaves spend the years — pure water.
