Saitō Tokugin
斎藤徳元  ·  17 poems
Spring
春立つやにほんめでたき門の松
haru tatsu ya / nihon medetaki / kado no matsu
twin pines stand auspiciously / as Japan celebrates / the dawning of spring
槍梅の散らしかかるこだちかな
yari ume no / chirashi kakaru / kodachi kana
scattered plum blossoms / drop upon the thrusting grove / of young trees
Summer
しし舞もせよやぼたんの花見酒
shishi mai mo / se you ya botan no / hanami-zake
dance that lion / dance— among peony blossoms / sipping blossom-viewing sake
あきなひか空ねも高き杜宇
akinai ka / sora ne mo takaki / hototogisu
that’s business, I guess / inflating the asking price / the cuckoo / will I ever tire / of your sharp call high in the sky / cuckoo
一はつの 花や夏野の きのうけふ
ichi hatsu no / hana ya natsuno no / kinō kyō
first iris blooms— / yesterday and today / the summer meadow
鮎膾藍より青き蓼酢かな
ayu namasu / ai yori aoki / tade zu kana
sweet fish salad / bluer than indigo / nettle vinegar
五月雨の晴れて犬なく日和かな
samidare no / harete inu naku / hiyori kana
the long rains clear— / a dog barks into / the fine weather
諸行無常聞くや林鐘の声
shogyo mujo / kiku ya rinsho no / koe
all things must pass— / so I hear in the tolling / of the summer bell
人魂か魂祭る野に飛ぶ蛍
hitodama ka / tamamatsuru no ni / tobu hotaru
a wandering soul? / in the fields of the bon festival / a firefly drifts
Autumn
身にしむや香炉の煙秋の風
mi ni shimu ya / karō no kemuri / aki no kaze
piercing into me— / smoke from the incense burner blown / by the autumn wind
今まではいきたはごとを月夜かな
ima made wa / ikita wa goto wo / tsukiyo kana
until just now / everything felt like a dream— / a moonlit night
Winter
唐人も渡るや霜の日本橋
toujin mo / wataru ya shimo no / nihonbashi
even the foreign merchants / cross it—Nihonbashi / in the frost
口切に時雨を知らぬ青茶かな
kuchikiri ni / shigure wo shiranu / aojya kana
the season's first tea— / this pale green, ignorant / of the winter rain
煙にもすすけず白し富士の雪
kemuri ni mo / susukenu shiroshi / Fuji no yuki
not even smoke / can darken it—white / snow on Fuji
たぎりてや湯玉たばしる霰釜
tagiri te ya / tama tobashiru / arare kama
how it boils— / hail beads scatter and fly / from the kettle
武蔵野の雪ころばしか富士の山
musashino no / yuki korobashi ka / Fuji no yama
Musashino's snow / rolling down, is it? / Mount Fuji
New Year
足もとのよきをぞとらんよめが萩
ashi moto no / yoki wo zo toran / yome ha hagi
you oughta pick / supple limbed bride clover / right underfoot
