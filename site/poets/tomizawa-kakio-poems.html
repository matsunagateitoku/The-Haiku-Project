Tomizawa Kakio
富沢赤黄男  ·  20 poems
Spring
椿散るああなまぬるき昼の火事
tsubaki chiru / aa namanuruki / hiru no kaji
Camellias fall. / Ah, what a lukewarm / fire in the daytime!
春は白い卵と白い卵の影と
haru wa shiroi / tamago to shiroi / tamago no kage to
Spring: / white eggs / and white eggs' shadows.
Summer
豹の檻一滴の水天になし
hyō no ori / itteki no mizu / ten ni nashi
The leopard's cage: / not a drop of water / in heaven.
炎天に跫音きえて哄笑はのこる
enten ni / ashioto kiete / kōshō wa nokoru
In the sweltering sky / the sound of footsteps fades / and the laughter remains.
ひとの瞳の中の蟻蟻蟻蟻蟻
hito no me no / naka ni ari ari / ari ari ari
Within her eyes / ant, ant, ant, ant, ant.
結論のごとく地に跼し蟇
ketsuron no / gotoku chi ni kyoshi / hikigaeru
Like a conclusion / it crouches on the ground— / a toad.
Autumn
枯葦を瞳につめこんでたちもどる
kareashi o / me ni tsumekonde / tachimodoru
Withered reeds— / I stuff them into my eyes / and trudge towards home.
彷徨へる馬郷愁となりて消えぬ
samayoeru / uma kyōshū to / narite kienu
A wandering horse, / turning in a longing / for home, vanishes.
秋風は蛇のぬけがらより白し
akikaze wa / hibe no nukegara / yori shiroshi
The autumnal wind / surpasses an empty snakeskin / in whiteness.
黄昏の喪章黒犬がうづくまる
tasogare no / moshō kuroinu ga / uzukumaru
In the evening dusk / a mourning badge: a black dog / crouches.
葡萄一粒一粒の弾力と雲
būdō hitotsubu / hitotsubu no / danryoku to kumo
Grapes, each one of them with / resilience, and clouds
雲にあるあかるさ葦にあるくらさ
kumo ni aru / akarusa ashi ni / aru kurasa
Brightness that lies / in the clouds: darkness / that lies in the reeds
Winter
冬天に牡丹のやうなひとの舌
tōten ni / botan no yōna / hito no shita
Under the winter sky / it looks like a peony: / the woman's tongue.
岩黝く憂愁の牡蠣うすびかる
iwa kuroku / yūshū no kaki / usubikaru
The rocks are dark / and a melancholy oyster / faintly gleams.
咳けば枯木の天も咳けり
shiwabukeba / kareki no ten mo / shiwabukeri
As I cough / heaven above the leafless trees / coughs too.
嘘吐いて光彩うつくしきつららかな
uso tsuite / iro utsukushiki / tsurara kana
Uttering a lie / with beautiful colours, / an icicle.
winter
蝶墜ちて大音響の結氷期
chō ochite / daionkyō no / keppyōki
As a butterfly / plummets, a thunderous crash / in the freezing season.
no-season
錐をもむ暗澹として錐をもむ
kiri wo momu / antan to shite / kiri wo momu
I keep rubbing an awl, / in the darkness of despair / I keep rubbing an awl.
善意？どこまでつづく零の環よ
zen'i? / doko made tsuzuku / zero no wa you
Good will? / How far do the rings / of zeros extend?
Unclassified
gunkan ga / shizundaumi no / oitaru kamome
An aged seagull / over waters where a warship foundered.
