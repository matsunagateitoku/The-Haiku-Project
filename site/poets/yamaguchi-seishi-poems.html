Yamaguchi Seishi
山口誓子  ·  24 poems
Spring
春潮やわが総身に船の汽笛
shunchō ya / waga sōshin ni / fune no fue
The spring tide: / piercing my entire body, / the whistle from a boat.
少女たるよろこび春の日に瞑る
shōjo taru / yorokobi haru no / hi ni metsumuru
Delighted to be born / a girl, in the spring sun / she closes her eyes.
Summer
七月の青嶺まぢかく溶鉱炉
shichigatsu no / aone majikaku / yōkōro
On a July day / near the green mountains, / a smelting furnace.
夏草に気罐車の車輪来て止る
natsu kusa ni / kikansha no sharin / kite tomaru
Amid the summer grass / the wheels of a steam engine / come to a standstill.
夏の河赤き鉄鎖のはし浸る
natsu no kawa / akaki tessa no / hashi hitaru
River in summer一 / a red iron chain, its end / soaking in the water.
水甕に浮べる蟻の影はなく
mizugame ni / ukaberu ari no / kage wa naku
In the water jar / floats an ant, and its shadow / is not.
声なりしやと炎天を顧みる
koe narishi ya / to enten wo / kaerimiru
Was that a voice? / Back toward the sweltering sky / I turn my face.
炎天の遠き帆やわがこころの帆
enten no / tōki ho ya waga / kokoro no ho
Under the flaming sky, / a distant sail: in my / heart, a sail.
昼見たる蟻を暗夜に思ひ出づ
hiru mitaru / ari wo anya ni / omoi-izu
In the daytime I saw / an ant, and it haunts my mind / in this darkness of night.
やさしさは殻透くばかり蝸牛
yasashisa wa / kara suku bakari / katatsumuri
The gentleness / pervades its shell一 / a snail.
昼寝の中しばしば釘を打ち込まる
hirune no naka / shibashiba kugi wo / uchikomaru
Into my midday nap, / again and again, someone / hammers a nail.
浪にあとかたもなし女と泳ぎしが
nami ni atokata / mo nashi onna to / oyogishi ga
In the waves no trace / remains, though I have swum there / with a woman.
死にければ闇たちこむる蛍籠
shi ni kereba / yami tachikomuru / hotaru kago
Since they died / darkness fills up the space / in the firefly cage.
青野ゆき　馬は片眼に　人を見る
Ao no yuki / uma wa kata me ni / hito wo miru
Going the green field, / the horse with one eye / looks at you.
匙なめて　童たのしも　夏氷
Saji namete / warabe tanoshi mo / natsu gori
Licking their spoons, / happy children! / summer ice.
鯉幟　風に折れ又　風に伸ぶ
Koi nobori / kaze ni ore mata / kaze ni nobu
Carp banners — / they fold, then stretch / in the wind.
Autumn
かりかりと蟷螂蜂の皃を食むとは
karikari to / tōrō hachi no / kao wo hamu
With a crunching sound / the praying mantis devours / the face of a bee.
秋風が通るに嬰児片眼をあき
aki kaze ga / tōru ni eiji / katame wo aki
An autumnal wind / passes by, and a little baby / opens an eye.
ひとり膝を抱けば秋風また秋風
hitori hiza wo / dakeba aki kaze / mata aki kaze
All alone, I hug / my knees...an autumnal wind, / again, autumnal wind.
夕焼や思ひかへして貝拾ふ
yūyake ya / omoi kaeshite / kai hirou
The sunset glow: / changing my mind, I pick up / a seashell.
秋の暮水中もまた暗くなる
aki no kure / suichū mo mata / kuraku naru
Evening in autumn一 / under the water also / it becomes dark.
Winter
冬河に新聞全紙浸り浮く
fuyu kawa ni / shinbun zenshi / tsukari uku
In the winter river / a whole sheet of newspaper, / soaked through, floating.
winter
スケートの紐むすぶ間も逸りつつ
sukeeto no / himo musubu ma mo / hayari tsutsu
while lacing up / my skates / growing
春
行く雁の啼くとき宙の感ぜられ
yuku kari no / naku toki chū no / kanzerare
Passing in the sky / wild geese call; that instant / a feel of the mid-air.
