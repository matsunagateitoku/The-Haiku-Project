Saitō Sanki
西東三鬼  ·  20 poems
Summer
右の眼に大河左の眼に騎兵
migi no me ni / taiga hidari no / me ni kihei
In the right eye, / an immense river: in the left / eye, a horseman.
算術の少年しのびなけり夏
sanjutsu no / shōnen shinoni / nakeri natsu
Over arithmetic / a youngster voicelessly / weeps—summer.
機関銃眉間に赤き花が咲く
kikanjū / miken ni akaki / hana ga saku
A machine gun— / in the middle of the forehead / red blossoms bloom.
五月の地面犬はいよいよ犬臭く
gogatsu no / jimen inu wa iyoiyo / inu kusaku
On the ground of May / the dog ever increasingly / smells of dog.
向日葵播く雲の上なる日を探す
himawari maki / kumo no ue naru / hi wo sagasu
I sow sunflower seeds / and look up above the clouds / to locate the sun.
身に貯へん金山の蝉の声
mi ni takuwaen / zenzan no / semi no koe
Could I store it / in myself: a mountainful / of cicadas’ screech!
荒園の力あつまり向日葵立つ
kōen no / chikara atsumari / himawari tatsu
Concentrating the strength / of the abandoned garden, / a sunflower stands.
Autumn
雄鶏や落葉の下に何もなき
ondori ya / ochiba no shita ni / nani mo naki
A rooster: / beneath the fallen leaves / there is not a thing:
倒れたる案山子の顔の上に天
taore taru / kakashi no kao no / ue ni ten
Lying collapsed. / a scarecrow, and high above / its face, the heavens.
石工若し散る石片が秋の花
ishiku wakashi / chiru sekihen ga / aki no hana
The stonemason is young, / scattering fragments of stone / like blooms of autumn.
秋の暮大魚の骨を海が引く
aki no kure / taigyo no hone wo / umi ga hiku
Autumn nightfall— / the skeleton of a huge fish / is drawn out to the sea.
海に足浸る三日月に首吊らば
umi ni ashi / hitaru mikazuki ni / kubi tsuraba
The ocean would soak / my feet, if the crescent moon / hanged me by the neck.
Winter
限りなく降る雪何をもたらすや
kagirinaku / furu yuki nani wo / motarasu ya
The endlessly / falling snow—I wonder what / it is bringing to me.
クリスマス馬小屋ありて馬が住む
kurisumasu / uma-goya arite / uma ga sumu
Christmas Day— / there stands a stable, and / a horse is in it.
鯨食って始まる孤児と医者の野球
kujira kutte / hajimaru koji to / ishi no yakyū
After eating whalemeat / orphans and a doctor start / a baseball game.
おつまでも笑ふ枯野の遠くにて
itsumademo / warau kareno no / tōku nite
The laughter lasts / forever in the distance / on the withered moor.
病者起ち冬が汚せる硝子拭く
byōsha tachi / fuyu ga yogoseru / garasu fuku
A patient rises / and wipes the window pane / soiled by winter.
対岸の人と寒風もてつながる
taigan no / hito to kanpū mote / tsunagaru
On the other shore, / a person; the cold wind / connects me to him.
鴉飛び立てり羽ばたく枯野男
karasu tobi / tateri habataku / kareno otoko
The raven has flown / away: flapping his wings / on the moor, a man.
病む顔の前の硝子に雪張りつく
yamu kao no / mae no garasu ni / yuki haritsuku
On the window pane / in front of an ailing face / snowflakes have stuck.
