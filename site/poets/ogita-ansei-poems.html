Ogita Ansei
荻田安静  ·  4 poems
Spring
雨の親やそだつる花の子を大じ
ame no oya ya / sodatsuru hana no / ko wo daiji
the rain—a parent / nurturing the blossoms / to be cherished
Summer
引きかへて蛇を人やのむ菖蒲ざけ
hikikaete / hebi wo hito ya nomu / shōbu zake
transformed entirely— / do people swallow snakes / in the iris wine?
おぼえぬや手の舞ひ足の文月よ
oboe nu ya / te no mai ashi no / fumizuki yo
unconsciously— / hands dancing, feet moving / on this July night
Autumn
あぢはひはからや高麗きくの酒
ajiwai wa / kara ya korai / giku no sake
the flavor— / is it bitter? Korean / chrysanthemum sake
