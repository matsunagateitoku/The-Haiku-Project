Kikaku
其角  ·  7 poems
Spring
月花ヲ医ス　閑素幽栖の　野巫の子有り
tsuki hana wo / isu kansoyūsei no / yabu no ko ari
curing moon and blossom — / child of the simple hermitage, / a village quack
鐘ひとつ　売れぬ日はなし　江戸の春
kane hitotsu / urenu hi wa nashi / Edo no haru
not a day without a bell sold — / Edo in spring
Summer
見る人も　廻り灯籠に　廻りけり
Miru hito mo / mawari-toro ni / mawari keri
The man looking at / revolving lamps also / went round.
さをとめや　汚れぬ顔は　朝ばかり
Saotome ya / yogorenu kao wa / asa bakari
Rice-planting girls — / unsoiled faces, only / in the morning.
蚊をやく　龍眼肉の　閨の私語
ka wo yaku / ryūganniku no / neya no sasame goto
burning mosquitoes — / longan fruit / whispered talk in the bedchamber
Autumn
闇の夜は　吉原ばかり　月夜かな
yami no yo wa / Yoshiwara bakari / tsukiyo kana
on a dark night — / only Yoshiwara / is moonlit
New Year
日の春を　さすがに鶴の　歩みかな
hi no haru wo / sasuga ni tsuru no / ayumi kana
the first day of spring — / and sure enough, the crane / walks as a crane should
