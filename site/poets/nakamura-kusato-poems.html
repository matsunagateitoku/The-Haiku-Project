Nakamura Kusatō
中村草田男  ·  21 poems
Spring
耕せばうごき憩えばしづかな土
tagayaseba / ugoki ikoeba / shizukana tsuchi
When I plough / it moves; when I pause to rest / it is still—the earth.
梅一連踏まれて大地の紋章たり
ume ichirin / fumarete daichi no / monshō tari
A plum blossom, / trampled, has become the insignia / of the earth.
生れて十日生命が赤し風がまぶし
arete tōka / inochi ga akashi / kaze ga mabushi
Ten days after birth / the baby’s life is reddish, / the wind is dazzling.
Summer
万緑の中や吾子の歯生え初むる
banryoku no / naka ya ako no ha / hae somuru
Myriad green leaves— / in their midst my baby / begins to cut his teeth.
夜の蟻迷えるものは孤を描く
yoru no ari / mayoeru mono wa / ko wo egaku
Ants in the nighttime: / the one that has lost its way / crawls in a circle
永久に生きたし女の声と蟬の音と
towa ni ikitashi / onna no koe to / semi no ne to
How I wish to live / forever! A woman’s voice / and a cicada’s cry.
炎熱や勝利の如き地の明るさ
ennetsu ya / shōri no no gotoki / chi no akarusa
Scorching heat: / like a victory, the brightness / of the earth.
何事もなかりし如き日盛なり
nanigoto mo / nakarishi gotoki / hizakari nari
Appearing as if / nothing had happened, the brightness / of the midday sun.
睡蓮や死ならぬもの以て肉浄めよ
suiren ya / shi naranu mono mote / niku kiyome yo
A water lily: / may something other than death / cleanse my body!
頭を伏せし蜥蜴と聴けり日の言葉
zu wo fuseshi / tokage to kikeri / hi no kotoba
With a lizard / the droops its head, I listen / to the words of the sun.
覇王樹立ち夕蟹走りわれ生れし
saboten tachi / yūkani hashiri / ware areshi
A cactus stood, / an evening crab scurried, / and I was born.
田を植ゑし　しづかな音へ　出でにけり
Ta wo ueshi / shizukana oto e / ide ni keri
To the quiet / sounds of planting rice, / I went out. / Grass-Cutting
Autumn
空は太初の青き妻より林檎うく
sora wa taisho no / aosa tsuma yori / ringo uku
In the sky there is / primeval blue, and from my wife / I receive an apple.
葡萄食ふ一語一語の如くにて
budō kū / ichigo ichigo no / gotoku nite
Eating grapes— / like one word, another word, / and still another.
三日月のせた水輪こちらへ来たがるよ
mikazuki nosete / mizuwa kochira e / kitagaru yo
Each carrying a crescent / moon, water rings are anxious / to reach me!
Winter
冬すでに路標にまがふ墓一基
fuyu sudeni / rohyō ni magau / haka ikki
Winter already— / Too much like a signpost, / A gravestone.
空瓶と老いし盲の冬日向
akibin to / oishi mekura no / fuyu hinata
An empty bottle / and an aged blind man / in the winter sunshine
記憶を持たざるもの新雪と跳ぶ栗鼠と
kioku wo motazaru / mono shinsetsu to / tobu risu to
Things that do not possess / memories: freshly fallen snow / and a leaping squirrel.
比喩もろとも信仰消えて枯野の日
hiyu morotomo / shinkō kiete / kareno no hi
The metaphors are / gone, and so is my faith… / sun over a moor.
玉菜の芯から微かな鶏鳴広漠たり
tamana no shin kara / kasukana keimei / kōbaku tari
From inside a cabbage / the faint crow of a rooster— / vast and desolate.
New Year
旧景が闇を脱ぎゆく大旦
kyūkei ga / yami wo nugi-yuku / ō-ashita
The familiar scene / slowly strips off its darkness / as the New Year dawns.
