Summer
夏川を越すうれしさよ　手に草履
Natsu gawa wo / kosu ureshisa yo / te ni zori
How joyful to cross / a summer river! / Sandals in hand.
山々を低く覚ゆる　青田かな
Yama yama wo / hikuku oboyuru / aota kana
All the mountains / seem lower, by / green ricefields.
更衣　野路の人はつかに白し
Koromogae / noji no hito wazu / kani shireshi
Change of garments — / on field paths people / look whiter.
羅に　遮る蓮の　にほひかな
Usumono ni / saegiru hasu no / nioi kana
By a thin silk / kimono screened, the / scent of lotus.
細脛に夕風さはる　たかむしろ
Hoso hagi ni / yu kaze sawaru / takamushiro
On the thin legs / a dusk breeze — / bamboo mat.
後家の君　たそがれ顔の　うちはかな
Goke no kimi / tasogare gao no / uchiwa kana
The widow — / her face at dusk, behind / a round fan.
風鈴や　花にはつらき風ながら
Furin ya / hana seiun tsuraki / kaze nagare
Windbells — / though for flowers the / wind was cruel.
