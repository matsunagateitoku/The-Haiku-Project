Kaneko Tōta
金子兜太  ·  25 poems
Spring
人体冷えて東北白い花盛り
jintai hiete / touhoku shiroi / Hanazakari
my body feels the chill / Tohoku covered in white / masses of flowers
梅咲いて庭中に青鮫が来ている
ume saite / niwajuh-ni aozame-ga / kite iru
the plum in bloom / blue sharks have come right in / into the garden
崩れ煉瓦に蝶執着すここスラム
kuzure renga ni / chō shūjakisu / koko suramu
Above the crumbled brick / a butterfly, its heart attached / here to the slums.
Summer
蛾のまなこ赤光なれば海を戀う
ga-no manako / sekkou nareba / umi-o kou
the moth's eyes / glow red, yes the ocean / is the place for me
彎曲し火傷し爆心地のマラソン
wankyoku shi / yakedo shi bakushinchi-no / marason
over the once twisted / and charred ground zero / a marathon
おおかみに螢が一つ付いていた
ookami-ni / hotaru-ga hitotsu / tsuite ita
on the wolf / a firefly / attached itself
なめくじり寂光を負ひ鶏のそば
namekujiri / jakkō wo oi / tori no soba
A slug / bathed in an ethereal glow / near a chicken
墓地も焼跡蝉肉片のごと樹々に
bochi mo / yakeato semi nikuhen no / goto kigi ni
The graveyard is burnt too: / cicadas, like pieces of fleash / on the trees.
山には枯畑谷には思惟なくただ澄む水
yama ni wa / karehata tani niwa shii naku / tada sumu mizu
On the hill, a withered farm; / in the valley, no cogitation. / Clear water is all.
蜉蝣むらがる橋燈に来て影を得る
kagerō muragaru / kyōtō ni kite / kage wo uru
Ephemerae swarming / At a bridge lamp— I arrive / And gain a shadow.
群に入る目高素早く幸福に
mure ni iru / medaka subayaku / kōfuku ni
It joins a group— / a killfish, swiftly / and happily.
平和おそれるや夏の石石炭蓆かぶり
heiwa osoreru ya / natsu no sekitan / mushiro kaburi
Is it afraid / Of peace? Coal in summertime / Covered with straw mats.
白い人影はるばる田をゆく消えぬために
shiroi hitokage / harubaru ta wo yuku / kienu tame ni
A white human figure / far, far away, walks on the farm / in order not to fade.
激論つくし街ゆきオートバイと化す
gekiron / tsukushi machi yuki / ōtobai to kasu
After a heated argument / I go out to the street / and become a motorcycle.
どれも口美し晩夏のジャズ一団
dore mo kuchi / utsukushi banka no / jazu ichidan
Every one of the mouths / is beautiful—late in summer / a jazz group.
銀行員等朝より螢光す烏賊のごとく
ginkoin-ra / asa yori keikō su / ika no-gotoku
bank clerks / from the morning glowing / fluorescent as squid
強し青年干潟に玉葱腐る日も
tsuyoshi seinen / higata ni tamanegi / kusaru hi mo
Strong are the youngsters / even on a day when onions / rot at the dry beach
Autumn
手が長くだるし赤茶けた製鋼煙
te ga nagaku / darushi akachaketa / seikōen
Like an arm overstretched / and tired, reddish brow smoke / rising from a steel mill.
異質のごと新しき松かさ草に正座
ishitsu no goto / atarashiki matsukasa / kusa ni seiza
Like something totally / alien, a fresh pine cone / sits upright on the grass.
首切る工場秋雲の水を運河に吐き
kubi kiru kōba / shūdon no mizu wo / unga ni haki
Factory dismissing the workers— / it vomits cloudy autumn water / into the canal.
Winter
中学生神語りおり雪積む藁
chūgakusei / kami katari ori / yuki tsumu wara
High school boys / are talking of God, while the snow / keeps piling up on the ricks.
死にし骨は海に捨つべし沢庵嚙む
shinishi hone wa / umi ni sutsu beshi / takuan kamu
Dead bones / must be dumped into the sea! / I chew a piece of takuan.
奴隷の自由という語寒卵皿に澄み
dorei-no / jiyū to iu go kantamago / sara ni sumi
A slave's freedom' –so says / the phrase: an egg in midwinter / serene on the plate.
あまたの街角街娼争い蜜柑乾く
amata no machikado / gaishō arasoi / mikan kawaku
Factory dismissing the workers— / it vomits cloudy autumn water / into the canal.
ガット下にはりつく反吐が冬の華
gādo shita ni / haritsuku hedo ga / fuyu no hana
Under the girders of the bridge / vomit is stuck, blooming / as winter's blossoms.
