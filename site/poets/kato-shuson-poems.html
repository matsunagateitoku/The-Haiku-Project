Katō Shūson
加藤楸邨  ·  21 poems
Spring
木木霞み遠ひかるもの鷺となりぬ
kigi kasumi / tōhikaru mono / sagi to narinu
Trees are in a hazeー / something gleaming in the distance / has become a heron.
雉子の眸のかうかうとして売られけり
From the hydrangea’s / shade a pair of eyes watch: / as I look, nothing.
A pheasant, with eyes / defiantly glaring, / is being sold.
Summer
蟻殺すわれを三人の子に見られぬ
ari korosu / ware wo sannin no / ko ni mirarenu
I kill an ant / and realize my three children / have been watching.
灼け砂に果てにしものの形ただし
yakezuna ni / hatenishi mono no / katachi tadashi
On the scorching sand / lies a lost life, with its shape / impeccably preserved.
紫陽花の蔭に目があり見ればなし
ajisai no / kage ni me ga ari / mireba nashi
From the hydrangea’s / shade a pair of eyes watch: / as I look, nothing.
Autumn
かなしめば鵙金色の目を負ひ来
kanashimeba / mozu konjiki no / hi wo oi ku
I grieve, and there comes / a shrike, with golden sunbeams / on its back.
枯るるもの枯れゆき林しづかなり
karuru mono / kare yuki hayashi / shizukanari
All that withers / has withered away, and the grove / reposes in calm.
屋上に見し朝焼のながからず
okujō ni / mishi asayake no / nagakarazu
From atop the roof / I gaze at the morning glow / that does not last long.
蟷螂に蟷螂のごとくわが手を立つ
tōrō ni / tōrō no gotoku / waga te wo tatsu
At a mantis / I brandish my hand--like / a mantis.
火を消すや心崖なす月の前
hi wo kesu ya / kokoro gake nasu / tsuki no mae
I put out the light. / In my heart a precipice / before the moon.
猫と生れ人間と生れ露に歩す
neko to umare / nengen to umare / tsuyu ni hosu
She was born a cat / and I was born a man一 / we walk in the dew.
見つつ過ぐ秋風が松の傷吹くを
mitsutsu sugu / akikaze ga matsu no / kizu fuku wo
Passing by, I see / an autumnal wind blowing / at a pine tree’s wound.
落葉地にとどくや時間ゆるみけり
ochiba chi ni / todoku ya jikan / yurumi-keri
A falling leaf: / the moment it reaches the ground / time slows down.
秋の風鶏の見るもの我に見えぬ
aki no kaze / tori no miru mono / ware ni mienu
The wind of autumn一 / a chicken gazes at something / I cannot see.
柚子匂ふすぐそこ死に目ひらけば
yuzu niou / sugu soko no shi ni / me hirakeba
The scent of citron / as i open my eyes to death / right beside me.
Winter
わが凭りし冬木戦車の音となる
waga yorishi / fuyuki sensha no / oto to naru
The winter tree / I am leaning on has turned / into a tank’s roar.
坂くだる寒き夕焼に腕を振り
saka kudaru / samuki yūyake ni / ude wo furi
I walk down a slope; / at the cold sunset glow / I shake my fist.
寒雷や今は亡き目を負ひて生く
kanrai ya / Ima wa naki me wo / oite iku
Thunder in midwinter: / the eyes of a dead friend / looking on, I live.
霜夜子は泣く父母よりはるかなるものを呼ぶ
shimoyo ko wa / naku fubo yori harukanaru / mono wo yobi
This frosty night a baby / cries, craving for something / far more than its parents.
雪の中鴉のむくろ目をあけゑゐる
yuki no naka / karasu no mukuro / me wo akeiru
Amid the snow / lies the corpse of a crow / with its eyes open.
鮟鱇の骨までいててぶちきらる
ankō no / hone made itete / buchikiraru
the anglerfish / split open / as if frozen to the bone
