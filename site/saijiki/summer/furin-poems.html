風鈴 Furin
 ·  4 poems
風鈴や　花にはつらき風ながら
Furin ya / hana seiun tsuraki / kaze nagare
Windbells — though for flowers the wind was cruel.
Buson
風鈴に　何処へも行かず　暑しけり
Furin ni / doko e mo yukazu / kuwashi keri
By a windbell, without going out, I spent the day.
Takahashi Awajijo
月さして　風鈴の影　生いけり
Tsuki sashite / furin no kage / umare keri
The moon shone, a windbell's shadow was born.
Kiyosaki Toshio
風鈴や　水面に残る　風のあと
Furin ya / minamo ni nokoru / kaze no ato
A windbell — on the water surface, a trace of the wind.
Mizutani Saiko
