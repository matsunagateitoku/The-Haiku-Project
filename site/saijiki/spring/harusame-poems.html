春雨にあらいてけづれ柳がみ
harusame ni / araite kezure / yanagi gami
Spring rain washing, combing the willow hair.
Anonymous
春雨やたしかに見たる石の精
harusame ya / tashika ni mi-taru / ishi no sei
Spring rain: I am certain I saw the spirits of stones.
Murakami Kijyō
世にふるも更に時雨の宿りかな
yo ni furu mo / sara ni shigure no / yadori kana
enduring this world while all along the winter rain falls on my shelter
Sōgi
春雨や枕崩るるうたひ本
harusame ya spring rains— / makura kuzururu the pillow of song books / utaihon collapses
spring rain— the pillow dissolves into the chant book
Kagami Shikō
春雨や小磯の小貝ぬるるほど
harusame ya / koiso no kogai / nururu hodo
spring rain― until little shells on the beach are moist
