Spring
出替りや幼な心に物あはれ
degawari ya / osana kokoro ni / mono aware
the servants change posts— / in a child's heart / a sudden sadness
うまず女の雛かしづくぞ哀れなる
umazu onna no / hina kashizuku zo / aware naru
a childless woman / tending her doll with care— / how moving
Summer
竹の子や兒（ちご）の歯ぐきのうつくしき
take no ko ya / chigo no haguki no / utsukushiki
bamboo shoots— / like the gums of a young child, / so beautiful
Autumn
浪うつてよせ来る勢子や花薄
• / • / •
beating waves / the hunter boy press near / flowering pampas
名月や煙這いゆく水のうへ
meigetsu ya / kemuri hai yuku / mizu no ue
harvest moon— / smoke crawls and drifts / along the surface of the water
黄菊白菊其外の名はなくもがな
kigiku shiragiku / sono hoka no na wa / naku mo ga na
yellow chrysanthemum, white chrysanthemum— / beyond these names / I wish there were none
名月や煙這いゆく水のうへ
meigetsu ya / kemuri hai yuku / mizu no ue
harvest moon— / smoke creeps along / over the water
角力とり並ぶや秋のから錦
sumo tori / narabu ya aki no / kara nishiki
sumo wrestlers / standing in rows—autumn / Chinese brocade
真夜中やふりかはりたる天の川
mayonaka ya / furi kawari taru / amano gawa
midnight— / and the Milky Way / has shifted overhead
名月や烟這ひゆく水のうへ
meigetsu ya / kemuri hoi yuku / mizu no ue
bright harvest moon— / smoke drifting and crawling / across the water
黄菊白菊その外はなくもがな
kigiku shiragiku / sono hoka wa / naku mo ga na
yellow and white chrysanthemums— / beyond these names / I wish for none other
はぜ釣るや水村山廓酒旗の風
haze tsuru ya / suison sankaku / shuki no kaze
fishing for goby— / water village, mountain pass, / flag wine in the wind
Winter
凩の吹きゆくうしろすがたかな
kogarashi no / fuki yuku ushiro / sugata kana
the wintry gust: / blowing along, seen / only from behind
ふとん着て寐たる姿や東山
futon kite / netaru sugata ya / Higashiyama
wrapped in quilts / lying there—the shape of / the Eastern Hills
凩の吹きゆくうしろすがたかな
kogarashi no / fukiyuku ushiro / sugata kana
the cold winter blast: / blowing past, leaving only / a retreating figure
ふとん着て寐たる姿や東山
futon kite / netaru sugata ya / Higashiyama
quilts pulled over— / sleeping in that shape: / the Eastern Hills
うめ一輪一りんほどのあたたかさ
ume ichi rin / ichirin hodo no / atatakasa
one plum blossom— / one blossom's worth / of warmth
布団着て寝たる姿や東山
futon kite shape of a man / netaru sugata ya sleeping covered with a blanket— / higashiyama Mt. East

New Year
元日やはれて雀のものがたり
ganjitsu ya / harete suzume no / monogatari
New Year's Day— / the sky clears and sparrows / chattering away
