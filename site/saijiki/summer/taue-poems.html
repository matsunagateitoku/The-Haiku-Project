風流の　初やおくの　田植うた
Furyu no / hajime ya oku no / taue uta
The source of sweet simplicity — a north province's rice-plant song.
Basho
遠里や　二筋三すぢ　田うゑ笠
To zato ya / futasuji misuji / taue gasa
A remote village — two, three lines of rice-plant hats.
Ryota
蕗の葉に　いわしを配る　田植かな
Fuki no ha ni / iwashi wo kubaru / taue kana
In butterbur leaves, sardines passed around — rice-planting.
Issa
田を植ゑし　しづかな音へ　出でにけり
Ta wo ueshi / shizukana oto e / ide ni keri
To the quiet sounds of planting rice, I went out. Grass-Cutting
