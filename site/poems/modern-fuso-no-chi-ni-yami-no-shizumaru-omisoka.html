New Year
ōmisoka
父祖の地に闇のしづまる大晦日
fuso no chi ni / yami no shizumaru / ōmisoka
In my forefathers’ land darkness rests in calm this New Year’s Eve.
Notes

父祖の地 (soso no chi) = ancestral land. Darkness settling on the ancestral land on New Year's Eve.
