Oshima Ryōta
大島蓼太  ·  7 poems
Spring
馬借りてかはるがはるに霞みけり
uma karite / kawarugawaru ni / kasumikeri
Summer
緑わく　夏山陰の　泉かな
Midori waku / natsu yama kage no / izumi kana
Green wells up — / the spring of a summer / mountain shade.
遠里や　二筋三すぢ　田うゑ笠
To zato ya / futasuji misuji / taue gasa
A remote village — / two, three lines of / rice-plant hats.
夏痩の　わが骨探る　寝覚かな
Natsu yase no / waga hone saguru / nezame kana
The summer thin / pries his bones, / awakening.
祇園会や　京は日傘の　下を行く
Gion e ya / kyo wa higasa no / shita wo yuku
Gion festival — / in the capital they go / under parasols.
山寺や　五色にあまる　花み堂
Yamadera ya / goshiki ni amaru / hana mido
Mountain temple — / more than five colours, / the flowery pavilion.
spring
春の日や門ゆく梵論の影法師
haru no hi ya / kado yuku bonro no / kage-boshi
a spring dayー / the shadow of a lay priest / pases by the gate
