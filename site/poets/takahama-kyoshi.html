Selected poems
草を摘む子の野を渡る巨人かな
kusa wo tsumu / ko no no wo wataru / kyojin kana
Over the children / picking herbs on the fields something / gigantic passes.
Spring · kusa wo tsumu
夕立を真横に走る白帆かな
yūdachi wo / mayoko ni hashiru / shiraho kana
The summer shower— / cutting straight through it, there goes / a white sailboat.
Summer · yūdachi
裏戸より借るべき家の菊を見つ
urado yori / karu-beki ie no / kiku wo mitsu
Through the back gate / of the house I am to rent / chrysanthemums are seen.
Autumn · kiku
流れ行く大根の葉の早さかな
nagare yuku / daikon no ha no / hayasa kana
Floating away— / the turnip leaves—and how / swiftly the go!
