The Haiku Project
Autumn
chōyō
重陽や椀の蒔絵のことごとし
chooyoo ya / wan no makie no / koto gotoshi
Chrysanthemum Festival - in the bowl this laquer image almost the same
Notes

椀の蒔絵のことごとし (wan no makie no kotogotoshi) = the lacquerwork on the bowl is elaborate. Elaborate lacquerwork bowls at the chrysanthemum festival.
