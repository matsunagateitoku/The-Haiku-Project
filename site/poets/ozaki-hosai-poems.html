Ozaki Hōsai
尾崎放哉  ·  23 poems
Spring
春が来たと大きな新聞広告
Spring is here— / so says a spacious / newspaper ad.
haru ga / kita to ōkina / shinbun kōkoku
切られる花を病人見てゐる
kirareru / hana wo byōnin / mite-iru
An ailing person / watches / a flower / being cut.
Summer
一日物云はず蝶の影さす
ichi-nichi / mono iwazu / chō no kage / sasu
Throughout the day / I did not speak a word: / the shadow of a butterfly / falls.
人をそしる心をすて豆の皮むく
hito wo soshiru / kokoro wo sute / mame no kawa muku
Trying to cast away / a slanderous heart / I shell the beans.
すばらしい乳房だ蚊がいる
subarashii / chibusa da / ka ga iru
Splendid breasts— / there is a mosquito.
Autumn
夕日の中へ力いっぱい馬を追いかける
yūhi no / naka e chikara ippai / uma wo oikakeru
Into the evening sun / with all my strength / I chase a horse.
柘榴が口あけたたはけた恋だ
zakuro ga / kuchi aketa / tawaketa koi da
A pomegranate / has opened its mouth— / an idiotic / love affair.
烏がだまつてとんで行った
karasu ga / damatte / tonde itta
The crow / without saying a word / flew away.
お祭り赤ん坊寝てゐる
The festival: / a baby / is asleep.
omatsuri / akanbō / nete-iru
月夜の葦がおれとる
tsukiyo no / ashi ga / ore-toru
Moonlit night: / a reed / is broken.
no-season
焼き場の煙突の大いさをあふぐ
yakiba no / entotsu no / ōisa wo augu
At a crematorium / I look high up / towards the chimney’s / immensity.
井戸の暗さにわが顔を見出す
ido no kurasa ni / waga kao wo / miidasu
In the darkness of a well / I recognize my face.
夜中の襖遠くしめられたる
yonaka no / fusuma tōku / shimeraretaru
Late at night / a sliding door / in the distance / is closed.
山の夕陽の墓地の空海はかたぶく
yama no yūhi no / bochi no sora umi e / katabuku
The sky above a mountain graveyard / in the evening sun / slants towards the ocean.
淋しいからだから爪がのび出す
sabishii karada kara / tsume ga nobi-dasu
From a lonely body / fingernails begin to grow.
うつろの心に眼が二つあいてゐる
utsurono kokoro ni / me ga futatsu / aite-iru
In the hollow mind / a pair of eyes / opens.
足のうら洗へば白くなる
ashi no ura / araeba / shiroku naru
As I wash the sole of my foot / it becomes white.
流れに沿うて歩いてとまる
nagare ni / sōte aruite / tomaru
With the flow of the stream / I walk / and pause.
畳を歩く雀の足音を知って居る
tatami wo aruku / suzume no ashioto wo / shitte-iru
The footsteps of a sparrow / walking on the tatami floor / sound familiar.
墓のうらに廻る
haka no ura ni / mawaru
To the back of a tombstone / I go round.
Unclassified
ichinichi / mono iwazu / chō no kage sasu
All day long / I don't say word: / A butterfly casts its shadow.
tsukemono oke ni / shio fure to / haha wa unda ka
"Shake some salt / In the pickle tub!" / Is that why my mother bore me?
winter
咳をしても一人
seki wo shite mo / hitori
even coughing / I'm alone
