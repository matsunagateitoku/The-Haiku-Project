hotaru
篝火も蛍もひかる源氏かな
kagaribi mo / hotaru mo hikaru / genji kana
Cresset Fires and Fireflies shining Genji
Notes

篝火 (kagaribi) = bonfire/torch; 蛍 = fireflies. Both illuminate Genji scenes. Reference to The Tale of Genji chapters "Fireflies" and "Torchlight."
