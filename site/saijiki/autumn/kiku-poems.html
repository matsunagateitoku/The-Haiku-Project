黄菊白菊其外の名はなくもがな
kigiku shiragiku / sono hoka no na wa / naku mo ga na
yellow chrysanthemum, white chrysanthemum— beyond these names I wish there were none
Hattori Ransetsu
裏戸より借るべき家の菊を見つ
urado yori / karu-beki ie no / kiku wo mitsu
Through the back gate of the house I am to rent chrysanthemums are seen.
Takahama Kyoshi

kigiku shiragiku / sono hoka wa / naku mo ga na
yellow and white chrysanthemums— beyond these names I wish for none other
Hattori Ransetsu
白菊のしづくつめたし花鋏
shiragiku no / shizuku tsumetashi / hana-basami
White chrysanthemums— how cold the dewdrops are on my garden shears!
Iida Dakotsu
たましひのしづかにうつる菊見かな
tamashii no / shizuka ni utsuru / kikumi kana
My soul moves along quietly among the flowers— a chrysanthemum exhibit.
