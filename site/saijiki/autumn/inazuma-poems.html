Kaedei Ryōtoku
いなづまにまばたきしたる枯木達
inazuma ni / mabataki shi taru / kareki tachi
At a flash of lighting they blinked their eyes一 the leafless trees.

Mukai Kyorai
稲妻す妻の来し方我行く方
inazuma su / tsuma no koshi kata / waga yuku kata
Lightning flashes where my wife has come from, whither I go.
