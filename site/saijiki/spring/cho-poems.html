chō no hane / yo ni mo uruwashi / ari-ra / kore wo hiku
Butterfly's wings, most beautiful in the world; ants pull them.
Ogiwara Seisensui

蝶の空七堂伽藍さかしまに

To the butterfly in the sky all buildings on the temple ground are upside down.
Kawabata Bosha

The butterfly, now dead, floats across the flowing water once again.
Ishida Hakyō
