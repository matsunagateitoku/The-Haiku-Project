Ranko
関東  ·  4 poems
Spring
蝶飛ぶや　小狐狂ふ　岡のはら
chō tobu ya / kogitsune kuruu / oka no hara
butterflies flying — / a small fox frolicking / on the hillside plain
Summer
四辻や　匂ひ吹きみつ　あやめの日
Yotsu tsuji ya / nioi fukimitsu / ayame no hi
A crossroad — / the wind brings their fragrance, / blue flags day.
白根牡丹や　只一輪の　盛り草の花
shirane botan ya / tada ichirin no / sakari kusa no hana
Shirane peony — / just one bloom, / a flower at its peak among the grasses
New Year
若菜野や　赤裳引きづる　雪の上
wakana no ya / akamo hikizuru / yuki no ue
the young-greens field — / a red skirt trailing / across the snow
