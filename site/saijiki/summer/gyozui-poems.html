行水の　女にほれる　烏かな
Gyozui no / onna ni horeru / karasu kana
The tub-bathing lady, he's fallen for — Mr. Crow.

行水や　月に吹かるる　あばら骨
Gyozui ya / tsuki ni fukaruru / abara bone
Tub-bath — in moonlight being blown, the ribs.

Tub-bath — as dusk falls, the pine's deep green.
