貞徳  ·  5 poems
Spring
霞きへ　まだらにありて　たらの年
kasumi kie / madara ni arite / tara no toshi
the mist dissolves — / dappled and lingering, / another year
花よりも　団子にありて　帰る雁
hana yori mo / dango ni arite / kaeru kari
more than the blossoms — / it's the dumplings that matter, / geese returning
これはこれはとばかり花の吉野山
kore wa kore wa to / bakari / hana no Yoshinoyama
oh — oh —" / there are no other words: / the blossoms of Yoshino
Summer
皆人の　夕だちや　雲の帯
mina hito no / yūdachi ya / kumo no obi
everyone caught — / in the evening shower, / a sash of cloud
Autumn
山の腰に　ひるねの種や　秋の月
yama no koshi ni / hirune no tane ya / aki no tsuki
at the mountain's waist — / seeds of an afternoon nap, / autumn moon
