Kiginsui
言水  ·  4 poems
Summer
しら雲を　吹き尽くしたる　新樹かな
shirakumo wo / fukitsukushitaru / shinjukana
having blown away / every last white cloud — / fresh young leaves
Autumn
綿の花　たまたま蘭に　似たるかな
wata no hana / tamatama ran ni / nitaru kana
the cotton flower — / by chance resembling / an orchid
芋洗ふ　女に月は　落ちにけり
imo arau / onna ni tsuki wa / ochi ni keri
washing the taro — / onto the woman / the moon has fallen
Winter
笹の葉に　西日のめぐる　時雨かな
sasa no ha ni / nishi-hi no meguru / shigure kana
on the bamboo leaves — / the western sun turning / through the winter drizzle
