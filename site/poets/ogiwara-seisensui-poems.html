Ogiwara Seisensui
荻原井泉水  ·  26 poems
Spring
毬を待つ少女等が手は林なして戦げり
mari wo matu / otome-ra ga te wa / hayashi nashite / soyogeri
Awaiting the ball, / maidens' arms / form a grove / and sway.
たんぽぽたんぽぽ砂浜に春が目を開く
tanpopo tanpopo / sunahama ni / haru ga me wo aku
Dandelions, / dandelions / on the sandy shore-- / spring / opens its eyes.
蝶の羽世にもうるわし蟻らこれを曳く
chō no hane / yo ni mo uruwashi / ari-ra / kore wo hiku
Butterfly's wings, / most beautiful in the world; / ants / pull them.
花があるのに花をさがしている蝶々
hana ga aru no ni / hana wo sagashiteiru / chōchō
Flowers are there, / and yet, / looking for flowers, / a butterfly.
うちの蝶としてとんでいるしばらく
uchi no cho toshite / tondeiru / shibaraku
As the butterfly of my house, / it flies about / for a while.
垣があるので隣のつばきうちにおちる
kaki ga aru node / tonari no tsubaki / uchi ni ochiru
Because the fence is there / my neighbour's camellia / falls / into my yard.
Summer
水あれば田に青空が深く鋤かれある
mizu areba / ta ni aozora ga / fukaku sukare aru
As there is water / in the rice paddy, / the blue sky / is deeply ploughed.
仏を信ず麦の穂の青きしんじつ
hotoke wo / shinzu mugi no / ho no aoki / shinjitsu
In Buddha / I believe: / wheat-ears' green / truth.
牡丹一弁一弁の動きつつ開きつつ姿ととのう
botan / ichiben ichiben no / ugoki-tsutsu hiraki-tsutsu / sugata totonou
Peony: / one petal, / another petal, / moving, / opening, / puts itself in order.
かごからほたる一つ一つを星にする
kago kara / hotaru / hitotsu hitotsu wo / hoshi ni suru
From the cage / fireflies / one by one / turn into stars.
暑い日の空白、白い蝶一羽にて通る
atsui hi no / kūhaku shiroi / chō ichiwa nite / tōru
Hot day's / hollowness: / white butterfly / all alone / passes.
Autumn
物売の老いし影夕日に伸びきりたり
mono-uri no / oishi kage / yūhi ni / nobikiri-tari
Pedlar's / aged shadow / in the setting sun / stretches to its limit.
空をあゆむ朗々と月ひとり
sora wo ayumu / rōrō to / tsuki hitori
In the sky / walk / serenely-- / the moon alone.
霧に霧をいでてくる友を待ちておる
kiri ni kiri wo / idete kuru tomo wo / machite oru
In the fog / for a friend to come out of the fog / I keep waiting.
石と石月夜寄りそう
ishi to ishi / tsukiyo / yorisou
A stone / and a stone / in the moonlit night / nestle against one another.
空をあゆむ朗朗と月ひとり
sora wo ayumu / rōrō to / tsuki hitori
Crossing the sky / so silvery and clear / the moon alone
Winter
荷がおろされて寒い馬よ雨降る
ni ga orosarete / samui uma yo / ame furu
The load taken down, / a chilly horse! / It rains.
夜が氷嚢の白い沈黙でお前と私
yoru ga hyō no / shiroi chinmoku de / omae to watshi
Night is / ice-bag's / white silence-- / you and I.
石のまろさ雪になる
ishi no marosa / yuki ni naru
Stone's plumpness / turns into snow.
Unclassified
sara wo ayumu / rōrō tsuki hitori
It walks the heavens / Radiantly the moon, alone.
chikara ippai naku ko to / naku tori to no asa
Morning with a baby crying with all its might / And a crowing rooster.
tsuma no tsuioku no / suppai mikan wo / suute iru
I suck at / The sour tangerine of / Memories of my wife.
gyōzui wo / sutete kosui no / sasanigori
From a bathing tub / I throw water into the lake— / slight muddiness appears.
no-season
日曜の釣人よ監獄の塀が映る水
nichiyō no turibito yo / kangoku no hei ga / utsuru mizu
Sunday fisherman! / Prison wall / mirrored in the water
力一ぱいに泣く児と啼く鶏との朝
chikara ippai ni / naku ko to / naku niwatori to no asa
A morning / of babies crying, / of roosters crowing, / with all their might.
空はさびしよ家あれば煙をあげよ
sora wa sabishi yo / ie araba / kemuri wo age yo
sorrowful sky / if there is a house out there / please lit a fire
