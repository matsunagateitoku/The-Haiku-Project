Yasuhara Teishitsu
安原貞室  ·  4 poems
Spring
これはこれはとばかり花の吉野
kore wa kore wa to / bakari hona no / yoshino yama
this is it, this is it! / that's the only thing to say / Mt. Yoshino
歌いくさ文武二道の蛙かな
uta ikusa / bunbu nido no / kawazu kana
the poetry war— / a frog of two ways: / pen and sword
Autumn
そちは何をなげきの森のよるの蝉
sochi wa nani wo / nageki no mori no / yoru no semi
what is it you grieve / in the forest of sorrow / cicada of the night?
New Year
こもよみこ餅煮んとつむ若菜哉
komo yo mi ko / mochi ni n to tsumu / wakana kana
"dear child with your basket"— / gathering greens to make / festival mochi
