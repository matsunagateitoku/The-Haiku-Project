Morotake
守武  ·  4 poems
Spring
飛梅　やからろがろしくも　神の春
tobiumé ya / kakarogaroshi ku mo / kami no haru
the flying plum — / how lightly it moves, / spring of the god
青柳の　まゆかく　岸のひたひかな
aoyagi no / mayukaku / kishi no hitahi kana
the green willow / draws its brows — / the forehead of the bank
花よりも　鼻にありけるに　ほひかな
hana yori mo / hana ni arikeru / nioi kana
more than the blossoms — / it was in the nose all along, / that fragrance
Summer
かさきぎや　今日久かたの　雨の川
kasakigi ya / kyō hisakata no / ame no kawa
umbrella trees — / today, after all this time, / rain on the river
