Issa
一茶  ·  18 poems
Spring
瘦せ蛙　負けるな一茶　是にあり
yasegaeru / makeru na Issa / kore ni ari
thin frog — / don't give up, Issa / is here
Summer
松陰や　寝茣蓙一つの　夏座敷
Matsu kage ya / negoza hitotsu no / natsu zashiki
The shade of pine trees — / one rush mat to sleep on, / summer parlour.
大猫の　どさりと寝たる　団かな
O neko no / dosari to netaru / uchiwa kana
The big cat, / with a thud, lay on it, / round fan. / Windbell
虫干に　猫も干されて　居たりけり
Mushiboshi ni / neko mo hosarete / itari keri
For insect airing / the cat, too, / being aired.
蕗の葉に　いわしを配る　田植かな
Fuki no ha ni / iwashi wo kubaru / taue kana
In butterbur leaves, / sardines passed around — / rice-planting.
一番に　乙鳥のくぐる　ちのわ哉
Ichiban ni / tsubame no kuguru / chinowa kana
First of all, / a swallow went through, / lalong wreath.
新茶の香　真昼の眠気　転じたり
Shincha no ka / ma hiru no nemuke / tenji tari
The scent of new tea — / noon-time sleepiness / has gone away.
灌仏の　御指の先や　暮の月
Kanbutsu no / oyubi no saki ya / kure no tsuki
At the bathed / Buddha's august finger-tip, / the dusk moon.
五十 甥 天窓をかくす　扇かな
gojū oi / tenmado kakusu / ōgi kana
fifty — / hiding the skylight / with a fan
やれ打つな　蝿が手を摺り　足を擦る
yare utsu na / hae ga te wo suri / ashi wo suru
don't swat it — / the fly is rubbing / its hands and feet together
Autumn
父あり て　明の見たし　草の露
chichi arite / asu mo mitashi / kusa no tsuyu
my father is here — / i want to see tomorrow too, / the dew on the grass
露の世は　露の世ながら　さりながら
tsuyu no yo wa / tsuyu no yo nagara / sari nagara
this world of dew — / yes, it is only a world of dew, / and yet... and yet...
Winter
雪の日や　故郷人の　ぶあしらひ
yuki no hi ya / furusato bito no / buashirai
a snowy day — / the cold reception / of home-village people
心からし　なの雪に降られけり
kokoro karashi / na no yuki ni / furarekeri
from the heart — / fallen upon by the snow / of the plain
沙汐浜を　反故にして　飛ぶ千鳥かな
sachio hama wo / hōgu ni shite / tobu chidori kana
leaving the sandy shore / as waste paper — / flying plovers
New Year
初夢に　故郷を見て　泪かな
hatsuyume ni / furusato wo mite / namida kana
in my first dream — / i saw my home village, / and wept
Unclassified
我と来て　遊べや親の　ない雀
ware to kite / asobe ya oya no / nai suzume
come play with me — / you, sparrow, / with no parent either
六十　踊る夜もなく　過ごしけり
rokujū / odoru yo mo naku / sugoshi keri
sixty — / not a single night / of dancing has passed
