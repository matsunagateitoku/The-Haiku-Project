夏帽や　今年銀座に　柳無し
Natsuba ya / kotoshi Ginza ni / yanagi nashi
Summer hats — this year on the Ginza, no willows.
Shimada Seiho
夏帽の日を照り返す　渚かな
Natsubo no / hi wo teri kaesu / nagisa kana
Summer hats — on a strand that reflects the sun.
Mizuhara Shuoshi
麦藁帽　たたよふ海の　紺のなか
Mugiwarabo / tadayou umi no / kon no naka
A wheat straw hat floats, on the ocean's deep blue.
Kadokawa Genyoshi
カンカン帽　ゆゑに目に立つ　頬骨なる
Kankambo / yue ni me ni tatsu / hobone naru
A hard straw hat — and so, prominent, the cheek bones.
Shinohara Bon
